If you're like me and want to understand the current state-of-the-art, see how different fields of robotics connect, get serious inspiration for your own research, or are simply curious about the field, this seminar is for you.

Time commitment was spiky. On weeks with no reports/quizzes due, I spent 15-20 hours per week. On the weeks where the reports and quizzes were due (each unit had report and quiz due the same week), this jumped to 30 hours per week.

On the whole I liked the course while noting big room for improvement.

Rating: 3 / 5
Difficulty: 4 / 5
Workload: 25 hours / week
